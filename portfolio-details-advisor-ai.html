Project Information

  • Role: UI/UX Designer
  • Client: Texas A&M University, Texas, USA

Project Overview

An AI-powered student advising platform that assists students with academic planning, course selection, and career guidance. Designed to provide personalized recommendations based on student data and academic policies.

Category

Challenges :

  • Students lacked an easy way to plan courses and track degree progress on mobile devices.
  • Advisors struggled to provide timely guidance to a large student population.
  • Complex program rules were difficult to visualize in a small screen layout.

Contributions :

  • Designed mobile-friendly dashboards with degree maps and progress bars for quick status checks.
  • Created intuitive navigation patterns (tab bars, swipe interactions) optimized for iOS and Android.
  • Built conversational AI chat for real-time Q&A, reducing dependency on advisor appointments.
  • Prototyped wireframes and interactive mobile flows in Figma and validated with student test groups.

Impact:

  • Reduced advising wait times by 40% through AI-guided self-service.
  • Increased student adoption with a 70% higher engagement on mobile vs web portals.
  • Improved clarity for degree requirements, boosting timely graduation rates.
