Project Overview

Developed an automation solution to streamline the Accounts Payable (AP) invoice and Purchase Order (PO) reconciliation process for Ace Parking and Essen Health Care, handling over 100,000 invoices annually.

Category

Challenges :

  • Finance teams faced high-volume invoice processing with frequent mismatches and delays.
  • Lack of real-time dashboards led to poor visibility and repeated submissions.
  • Legacy web forms were not fully accessible.

Contributions :

  • Designed & developed a fully accessible invoice upload and approval UI integrated with NetSuite and PeopleSoft ERP.
  • Created dashboards for real-time tracking, document previews, and multi-step PO matching workflows.
  • Reduced user entry errors by 50% and improved processing efficiency by up to 60% via form enhancements, validation states, and in-app notifications.
  • Achieved WCAG 2.1 AA compliance across all modules using axe DevTools, Lighthouse, and screen reader testing (NVDA, VoiceOver).
  • Conducted user research with AP teams to identify pain points in legacy workflows and translated findings into streamlined UX flows and feature prioritization.
  • Designed and prototyped critical interactions (drag-and-drop uploads, approval modals, audit trails) using Figma, ensuring alignment with business rules and user goals.

Impact:

  • Finance users enjoyed quicker invoice approvals, reduced mismatches, and enhanced visibility throughout the process.
  • Introduced contextual tooltips, progress indicators, and micro-interactions to guide users through complex invoice steps, reducing abandonment rates.
  • Integrated real-time status updates via WebSockets for high-volume invoice syncs, improving user confidence and reducing duplicate submissions.
